OneProfile™

One secure safety profile. One scan when it matters.

RATIOS OneProfile™ is a future secure family safety profile designed to help caregivers organize important information and share emergency details through QR codes, NFC tags, safety cards, and future LifePatch™ products.

Join the WaitlistView Sample Profile

What it is

Built to help families prepare before an emergency.

OneProfile™ gives families one organized place to keep the details that matter most: medical notes, emergency contacts, communication preferences, sensory needs, comfort strategies, and safety instructions.

Secure family login is currently in development. This page is a preview of the future RATIOS OneProfile™ experience.

How families use it

1

Create a profile

Add key details about your loved one, including photo, preferred name, emergency contacts, medical notes, and communication style.

2

Connect safety products

Use QR/NFC cards, tags, stickers, patches, or printed sheets to connect responders to a simplified emergency view.

3

Update anytime

Families can keep emergency contacts, medications, caregiver notes, triggers, and calming strategies current.

4

Share when needed

In an emergency, first responders and trusted caregivers can quickly understand how to help with dignity and care.

What can be stored

Important details in one place.

OneProfile™ is designed around practical information families already share repeatedly with schools, caregivers, hospitals, programs, and first responders.

  • Photo and preferred name
  • Emergency contacts
  • Medical conditions and medications
  • Communication style and processing needs
  • Likes, comforts, and calming strategies
  • Sensory triggers and safety risks
  • Wandering or elopement risk notes
  • Emergency instructions

QR + NFC connection

Safety tools that connect to a profile.

QR Safety Card

A printed card families can place in a wallet, backpack, binder, or emergency folder.

NFC Tag

A tap-to-open option for compatible smartphones, cards, tags, or future wearable products.

LifePatch™

A future dignity-centered patch concept connecting responders to the right information quickly.

Emergency Sheet

A printed profile families can share with schools, day programs, caregivers, and first responders.

Common questions

OneProfile™ FAQ

Is OneProfile™ live now?

Secure family login is currently in development. Families can join the waitlist now.

Will families control what is shared?

Yes. The goal is for families to decide what appears on the emergency view and what stays private.

Can QR or NFC products connect to it?

Yes. OneProfile™ is being designed so QR cards, NFC tags, LifePatch™ products, and printed sheets can connect to emergency information.
