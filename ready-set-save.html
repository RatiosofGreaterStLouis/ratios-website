Ready, Set, Save!

Confidence to respond when every second matters.

Ready, Set, Save! brings practical CPR, BLS, AED, first-aid, and emergency preparedness education to children, teens, adults, families, caregivers, schools, churches, and community groups.

Hands-on learning

Skills people can use with confidence.

Workshops combine demonstrations, hands-on practice, plain-language instruction, and realistic emergency scenarios so participants leave feeling more prepared—not overwhelmed.

  • Adult and child CPR practice
  • AED awareness and guided use
  • Age-appropriate youth safety education
  • Family and caregiver preparedness
  • Community, school, church, and workplace workshops

Workshop options

Training designed for different ages and settings.

Programs can be adapted for community groups, schools, caregivers, faith-based organizations, and family events.

CPR

Hands-Only CPR

Simple, memorable instruction focused on recognizing cardiac arrest and beginning strong chest compressions.

AED

AED Awareness

Participants learn what an AED does, how to follow its prompts, and how it supports emergency response.

BLS

Basic Life Support

Structured education for healthcare learners, caregivers, staff, and community members seeking deeper skills.

KIDS

Kids Safety

Age-appropriate emergency concepts, calling for help, recognizing danger, and building confidence without fear.

FA

First Aid Basics

Practical guidance for common injuries, bleeding, choking, and what to do while professional help is on the way.

FAM

Family Preparedness

Planning tools for home, school, community events, emergency contacts, medications, and communication needs.

Inside the classroom

Training that is active, welcoming, and practical.

Building confident lifesavers.

Participants practice, ask questions, and leave with clear next steps they can remember in an emergency.
