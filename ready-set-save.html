Ready, Set, Save!

Safety education that feels approachable, practical, and empowering.

One-day safety workshops for ages 4–80.

Ready, Set, Save! brings emergency readiness education into schools, community centers, churches, and family-friendly settings. The program can include age-appropriate CPR awareness, first aid basics, emergency response conversations, safety games, and take-home materials.

Workshop topics can include

  • When and how to call for help
  • Basic emergency readiness
  • Safety at home, school, and in the community
  • Caregiver and child-friendly activities
  • Hands-on demonstrations and confidence building
