Ready, Set, Save!

Safety education for every family, every background, and every community.

Ready, Set, Save! is a one-day workshop concept designed for children, adults, caregivers, schools, churches, nonprofits, and community groups. The program is intentionally inclusive and welcoming to interracial families, multicultural households, grandparents raising grandchildren, foster families, blended families, and families caring for loved ones with disabilities or memory-related conditions.

Who it is for

Built to represent the real families in our community.

RATIOS serves families across Greater St. Louis and beyond. Our website visuals and workshops should reflect the full community: Black families, white families, interracial families, Latino families, Asian families, multigenerational families, disability families, and blended households.

Workshop topics

Memorable safety skills without fear-based teaching.

  • Basic life support awareness and emergency response confidence
  • When and how to call for help
  • Choking, CPR awareness, and first aid basics
  • Emergency ID information and caregiver preparation
  • How to support someone who cannot clearly communicate
  • Inclusive safety planning for autism, Down syndrome, dementia, Alzheimer’s disease, FASD, intellectual disabilities, and communication differences
