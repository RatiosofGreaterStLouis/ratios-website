Ready, Set, Save! A RATIOS Program

Confidence to respond when every second matters.

Ready, Set, Save! brings practical CPR, BLS, AED, first-aid, and emergency preparedness education to children, teens, adults, families, caregivers, schools, churches, and community groups.

Request a Workshop Sponsor a Class
Hands-on learning

Skills people can use with confidence.

Our workshops combine demonstrations, plain-language instruction, hands-on practice, and realistic scenarios so participants leave feeling more prepared to help someone in need.

  • Adult and child CPR practice
  • AED awareness and guided use
  • Age-appropriate youth safety education
  • Family and caregiver preparedness
  • Community, school, church, and workplace training
