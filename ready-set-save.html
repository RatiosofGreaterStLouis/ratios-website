Ready, Set, Save!

Emergency readiness that feels approachable for every age.

Ready, Set, Save! is RATIOS’ community BLS and emergency readiness education program for ages 4–80.

What participants learn

Recognize an emergency

How to stay calm, get help, and tell a safe adult or responder what happened.

Basic life support awareness

Age-appropriate CPR, AED, choking response, and safety basics taught by qualified instructors.

First-aid readiness

Simple skills, take-home safety reminders, and family emergency planning.

Bring Ready, Set, Save! to your group.

Schools, campuses, churches, community centers, and organizations can request a workshop or presentation.
