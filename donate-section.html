Every Journey Deserves Protection.

When a child with autism wanders, when an older adult with dementia goes missing, or when an individual with a developmental or communication disability becomes separated from their caregiver, every second matters.

Ratios exists to help make those seconds count.

Through identification tools, caregiver education, emergency preparedness resources, community outreach, and partnerships with first responders, Ratios works to help vulnerable individuals remain Recognized. Protected. Connected.

Help Us Protect the Next Journey.

Every contribution helps provide LifeCards™, LifePatches™, LifeBands™, identification kits, wandering-prevention resources, and emergency support tools to families who need them.
