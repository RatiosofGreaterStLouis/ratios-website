Our Purpose

Every person deserves to be recognized.

RATIOS helps families, caregivers, educators, healthcare professionals, first responders, and community partners prepare for moments that matter.

Through education, practical resources, partnerships, and advocacy, we work to reduce barriers, strengthen communication, and improve safety while protecting the dignity and individuality of every person we serve.

Service area

Starting in Greater St. Louis. Built to grow.

RATIOS begins with families, schools, caregivers, and first responders in the Greater St. Louis region, with a vision to expand outreach, safety education, and community response partnerships.

  • St. Louis City and North County outreach
  • Schools, churches, community centers, and health fairs
  • First responder and caregiver education
  • Multicultural, inclusive, and dignity-centered support
