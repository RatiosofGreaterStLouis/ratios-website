Safety Kits

Small tools can make a major difference in an emergency.

RATIOS safety kits are designed to help someone be identified, understood, and reunited with their caregiver faster.

Kit contents may include

  • Emergency ID card
  • Caregiver contact sheet
  • Communication profile
  • Recent photo guidance
  • Medical and sensory notes
  • Wearable ID, labels, or bag tags when available
  • Wandering prevention checklist
  • First responder information sheet
