RATIOS Safety Kits

Prepared information can save precious time.

Safety kits are designed to help families organize important information before a loved one wanders, becomes lost, or needs emergency assistance.

Kit components

  • Personal ID card template
  • Recent photo guidance
  • Emergency contacts
  • Communication needs
  • Medical or sensory considerations
  • Known triggers or calming supports
  • Favorite places or wandering patterns
  • Caregiver planning checklist
  • First responder information sheet
  • Basic first-aid resources as available

RATIOS tools are not a replacement for emergency services. Always call 911 when someone is missing, injured, or in immediate danger.
