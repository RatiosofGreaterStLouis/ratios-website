Safety Kits

Identification can save precious time.

RATIOS Safety Kits are designed to help caregivers prepare, responders identify, and communities act quickly.

One of One

Kit contents

  • Emergency identification card
  • Caregiver contact and communication form
  • Recent photo and physical description template
  • Medical, behavioral, and sensory needs section
  • Awareness wearables or printed resources
  • Emergency action checklist
