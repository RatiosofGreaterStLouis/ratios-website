Ready, Set, Save!

Safety education for families of every background.

Ready, Set, Save! brings approachable safety education to children, teens, adults, families, caregivers, and community groups across multicultural communities.

Learn. Practice. Respond.

Workshops can include basic emergency response, age-appropriate CPR/BLS awareness, when to call 911, how to identify danger, and how to help someone who may be lost, scared, or unable to communicate.

  • School and youth group workshops
  • Family-friendly safety education
  • Community health events
  • Caregiver and volunteer training
