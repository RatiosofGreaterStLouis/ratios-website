Programs

Practical support across the people and places where safety matters most.

RATIOS programs are designed for families, caregivers, schools, first responders, and community partners.

Identification & Safety Kits

Emergency ID cards, caregiver contacts, photos, communication information, and safety planning resources.

Explore kits →

Wandering Prevention

Resources for individuals who may wander due to autism, Down syndrome, Alzheimer’s disease, dementia, fetal alcohol spectrum disorders, intellectual disabilities, brain injury, or communication barriers.

Caregiver Support

Education, planning tools, resource referrals, and community support for families who need practical safety planning.

First Responder Awareness

Training and awareness resources for police, EMS, firefighters, dispatchers, and community safety teams.

School & Youth Outreach

Education for students, staff, and school communities on emergency communication, identification, and inclusion.

Ready, Set, Save!

Basic life support and safety workshops designed for ages 4–80 with engaging, community-centered teaching.
