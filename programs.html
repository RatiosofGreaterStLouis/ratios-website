Programs & initiatives

Support designed for real-life situations.

ID

Identification & Safety Kits

Emergency ID cards, caregiver forms, current-photo templates, communication details, and family planning tools.

QR

OneProfile™

A developing secure profile concept designed to connect QR and NFC products with essential emergency information.

WP

Wandering Prevention

Planning support for homes, schools, transportation, public places, and community events.

FR

First Responder Outreach

Education that supports calm, informed, and person-centered interactions during emergencies.

SP

School Partnerships

Awareness resources, family communication tools, safety education, and community resource tables.

RS

Ready, Set, Save!

Approachable CPR, emergency response, and safety learning for children, families, caregivers, and community groups.
