Why It Matters

Seconds matter.

When someone vulnerable becomes separated from their caregiver, preparation can change the outcome.

RATIOS helps families and communities prepare before a child with autism wanders, an older adult with dementia becomes lost, or a person with a developmental or communication disability goes missing.

Recognized.Identification resources help others know who they are.
Protected.Safety planning helps families respond with confidence.
Connected.Community partnerships support faster reunification.
